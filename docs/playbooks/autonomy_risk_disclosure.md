# Autonomy Risk Disclosure

## System Overview

The [Platform Name] Marketing Intelligence Architecture utilizes advanced deterministic algorithms, statistical modeling, and bounded intelligence frameworks to analyze, diagnose, and execute marketing adjustments. This closed-loop system is designed to optimize media spend, detect creative fatigue, and adapt to macro-market volatility continuously.

## Scope of Autonomous Actions

By enabling `Autonomous Execution`, you authorize the system to interact with designated third-party advertising APIs (e.g., Meta, Google) on your behalf, utilizing the explicit `Genesis` constraint frameworks established by your organization.

These autonomous bounded adjustments include:

* Dynamic budget scaling operations within your explicitly defined `max_budget_change_percent`.
* Creation and assignment of sandbox testing environments for creative iteration logic.
* Pausing or deprecating specific ad creatives categorized mathematically as fatigued or decaying.

**The system does not utilize generative, unconstrained "AI hallucination" mechanisms for execution routing; all actions are derived from strictly enforced deterministic logic structures.**

## Risk Mitigation Controls

The platform operates under a system of absolute governance parameters:

* The system operates bounded by absolute limits, and will never overspend the explicit `max_daily_budget` parameter designated in the liquid governance file.
* The system uses contextual models to detect external market turbulence ("Pulse"), artificially dampening confidence thresholds internally if auction volatilities bypass safe metrics.
* Internal scaling parameters must securely pass strict statistical variance checks explicitly.

## Rollback & Override Rights

* **Automated Reversal Mechanics:** The system executes continuous 24-to-48 hour performance monitoring post-execution. If the resulting financial impact deviates negatively from projected baseline mathematics beyond acceptable variance, the system triggers an automatic `Rollback` to safely revert the modifications instantly.
* **Continuous Client Override Rights:** Your organization maintains absolute continuous authority over the intelligence structure. At any time, operations can be halted immediately by reverting the configuration state safely.

## Performance Variability Disclaimer

While the autonomous system is built to consistently maximize mathematical precision over human-operated historical baselines, digital advertising markets inherently contain uncontrollable volatility. Mathematical predictions of expected return-on-ad-spend (ROAS) and cost-per-acquisition (CPA) represent dynamic probabilistic projections, not explicitly guaranteed financial outcomes. The client acknowledges that autonomous execution carries the intrinsic risk of temporary performance degradation driven inherently by macro market behaviors beyond the software's direct jurisdiction.

## Data Handling & Security

All executed decisions and point-in-time mathematical constraints are permanently logged within an immutable audit architecture providing continuous traceability mapping safely. Third-party authentication tokens are securely encrypted structurally and utilized strictly for validated execution routing.
