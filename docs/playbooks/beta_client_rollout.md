# Beta Client Rollout Framework

## 1. Client Selection Criteria

The successful rollout of the Autonomous Marketing OS depends fundamentally on introducing the system to the correct baseline environment.

### Mandatory Beta Client Parameters

* **Stable Historical Performance:** Client must lack wild baseline conversion swings implicitly.
* **Low Volatility Vertical:** Avoid rapid flash-sale, highly volatile event-driven operations initially. (E.g. select Evergreen SaaS or stable D2C over Drop-shipping).
* **Budget Range Suitability:** Enough budget to exit statistical noise (e.g. > $15K/mo), but within safe liability bounds.
* **Trust Relationship Strength:** The client must be bought-in to the strategic evolution of the intelligence architecture, strictly understanding beta boundaries.
* **Data Quality Requirements:** Perfect Pixel/API tracking fidelity. Atlas cannot navigate blind platforms.

## 2. 14-Day Shadow Mode

Initial integration does not interact with the client's money structure in any physical way.

* **Silent Parallel Run:** The system ingests their API tokens and fully activates Atlas, Hawkeye, and Pulse internally.
* **No Execution:** `shadow_mode_enabled` is locked on. Captain produces explicit optimization arrays natively.
* **Decision Comparison vs Human Strategy:** We measure our expected simulated ROI vs the actions the client's current human team actually took.
* **Divergence Tracking:** We passively track execution variance logs accurately.
* **Precision Scoring:** We expose the Sentinel Score explicitly to the client showing them exactly how we outperformed/matched their team on a theoretical ledger.

## 3. Gradual Autonomy Activation

Once Shadow Mode proves safety, physical routing is engaged cautiously.

* **Stage 1 (Sandbox Only):**
  * We allocate a strict 5–8% budget cap assigned directly to Forge natively.
  * System begins executing `CREATIVE_ANGLE_SHIFT` tests and verifying statistical significance autonomously without touching their core campaign structures.
* **Stage 2 (Controlled Scaling Actions):**
  * CaptainExecute is un-silenced for specific `SCALING_OPPORTUNITY` diagnosis routes.
  * Limited exclusively to Evergreen specific campaign types.
* **Stage 3 (Full Bounded Autonomy):**
  * The entire architecture (Fatigue killing, macro dampening, budget scaling) is active. The system operates entirely under their requested Genesis Profile constraints logically.

## 4. Communication Plan

Transparency of the deterministic safety architecture is our primary defense against client anxiety.

* **Autonomy Boundaries:** We educate that the system is bounded by math (Genesis Liquid constraints) and not "Generative AI Guesswork."
* **Risk Containment:** We explain that Genesis globally restricts any adjustment beyond strict parameter limits.
* **Rollback Safeguards:** We demonstrate the Sentinel ExecutionMonitor mechanism that physically reverses bad optimizations automatically seamlessly.
* **Confidence Thresholds:** We explain how Pulse natively reduces scaling algorithms if it senses Macro market turbulence explicitly.
* **Drift Monitoring:** We frame our system as highly self-aware, constantly tuning its own bias mapping internally.

## 5. Beta Success Criteria

The Beta exits validation upon satisfying the following:

* **Performance Lift:** Definitive positive ROAS/CPA drift vs baseline historicals.
* **Risk Reduction:** Less catastrophic ad spend waste over weekends/nights.
* **Decision Latency Reduction:** The time from 'Creative Fatigues' to 'New Creative Scaled' drops from weeks to hours explicitly.
* **Human Oversight Reduction:** Account managers spend 0% time adjusting budgets and 100% time discussing macro-strategy.
* **Client Confidence Index:** Complete client willingness to authorize Stage 3 (Full Autonomy) bounds natively.
