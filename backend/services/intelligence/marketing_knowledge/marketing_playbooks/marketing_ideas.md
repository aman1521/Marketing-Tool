# Idea Generation

When growth hits a complete ceiling, run through structured ideation methods to rethink the problem space.

## 1. The "Reverse" Brainstorm

Instead of asking "How do we get more signups?", ask "How do we make absolutely sure nobody ever signs up for our product?"

1. List all the worst possible actions.
2. Invert them into positive actions.
3. Often uncovers hidden friction you didn't realize you were already doing (e.g. "Ask for a credit card before they see the dashboard").

## 2. Analogous Industry Mapping

1. Look at a completely different industry (e.g. if you are B2B SaaS, look at high-end fitness clubs).
2. How do they onboard? How do they handle churn?
3. Adapt their physical/service models into your digital flow.
