# Engineering as Marketing

Building a free mini-tool that captures leads passively for years.

## The Framework

1. **Identify the Core Value**: What is the singular most painful calculation or lookup your target personas do every week?
2. **Build the Tool**: Construct a one-page calculator, grading system, or generator. (e.g., A/B Test Significance Calculator for marketers).
3. **Gating**: Keep the initial result free. Gate the "export" or "detailed breakdown" behind an email capture.
4. **Distribution**: Launch on Product Hunt, Hacker News, Reddit logic subreddits.

## Output

- mini_tool_concept
- target_persona
- gating_strategy
