# Growth Experiments

## Experiment Structure

1. **Hypothesis**: The core belief driving the test. E.g., "Changing the CTA copy to 'Get Free Trial' will increase conversions by 15%."
2. **Experiment Design**: A/B vs Multivariate testing, specific traffic split methodology, audience segment to target.
3. **Metrics**: The primary success metric (e.g. Lead CVR) vs secondary balancing metrics (e.g. Bounce Rate, Time on Site).
4. **Expected Outcome**: What changes to anticipate.

## Output

- formalized hypothesis string
- required tool integration
- duration to reach statistical significance
