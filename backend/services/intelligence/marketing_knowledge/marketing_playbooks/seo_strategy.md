# SEO Strategy

## Steps

1. Keyword Research: Identify high-volume, low-competition keywords targeting "intent".
2. Technical Auditing: Crawl the site for 404s, slow pages, and missing meta tags.
3. Content Clusters: Group related articles around primary "Pillar Pages" to signal authority.
4. Backlink Strategy: Identify link-building opportunities through competitor analysis.

## Frameworks

### The Skyscraper Technique

- Find the top-performing content for your keyword.
- Build something significantly better, more comprehensive, or visually appealing.
- Reach out to the people linking to the original content to update their links to yours.

### Topic Clusters

- Create an exhaustive central Pillar Page.
- Create 5-10 related Cluster Pages.
- Interlink them systematically to pass SEO authority.
