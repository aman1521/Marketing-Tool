# Copywriting Frameworks

When standard ad creatives and landing pages fail due to saturation, fall back on proven psychological frameworks to rewrite the primary and secondary messaging.

## Frameworks

### 1. AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)

- **Attention**: Grab the reader's eye with a bold statement or question.
- **Interest**: Keep them reading with fascinating facts or relatable problems.
- **Desire**: Make them want what you have by highlighting the core benefits.
- **Action**: Tell them exactly what to do next to get it.

### 2. PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)

- **Problem**: Identify a painful problem the target audience experiences.
- **Agitation**: Make the problem visceral by describing its negative impact.
- **Solution**: Present the product as the exact antidote to the identified pain.

### 3. Feature, Benefit, Outcome

- **Feature**: What it is (e.g. 500GB SSD).
- **Benefit**: What it does (e.g. Loads files instantly).
- **Outcome**: What this means for the user's life (e.g. Never wait on a screen again, get home to your family faster).

## Output

- Rewrite of the landing page or ad variants using these frameworks.
